FOR KIDS

Embark on an Exciting Science Adventure at Our Interactive Museum!

Calling all young science enthusiasts! If you're eager to expand your knowledge and dive into the fascinating world of science, then our museum is the perfect destination for you. With a wide range of interactive exhibits and hands-on activities, there's something for everyone to enjoy.

Discover the Wonders of Physics and Prehistory at Our Engaging Museum Exhibits!

Step into the realm of physics as you experience the thrill of our bumper swing and learn about Newtonian principles in action. Or, take a journey back in time and come face-to-face with our resident dinosaur, uncovering the mysteries of prehistoric life along the way. Our exhibits are designed to spark curiosity and inspire exploration, so come prepared to immerse yourself in a world of discovery.

Join the Young Stars Club: Ignite Your Passion for Science Through Hands-On Exploration!

But that's not all – for young aspiring scientists eager to delve deeper into the realms of experimentation and discovery, we offer the Young Stars club. Held once a week on Saturdays from 10:00 to 13:00, this exclusive club provides a unique opportunity to work alongside our team of experts, engaging in hands-on experiments, interactive workshops, and educational activities designed to ignite your passion for science.

Unleash Your Curiosity: Holiday Clubs for Adventurous Young Explorers!

And when school's out for the holidays, the fun doesn't stop! Join us for our special holiday clubs, where you'll have the chance to embark on thrilling journeys of exploration with other children your age. Each holiday brings a new theme to explore, from astronomy to ecology and everything in between. To learn more about our holiday club and how you can join in on the adventure, simply send us a message – we'd love to hear from you!

FOR TEACHER

Collaborate with Us: Enhancing Education through Interactive Museum Experiences!

As a community-driven museum, we want to work with schools to create places of learning and exploring. Our team are on hand to give your students guided tours of the museum, teach them in our learning laboratory, and provide great video presentations that will excite and inspire them. Please get in touch with us here to find out more about our facilities and to arrange a time to bring your students through for a visit.

FOR RESEARCHERS

Collaborate with Researchers: Access Museum Resources!

Are you looking to get involved with our team of researchers and academics? Our museum offers various ways for you to use our resources and contribute towards them.We have online records, laboratory space, and a working relationship with a number of universities around the country.
