WELCOME TO
SCIENCE MUSEUM

Come joinus at the Community Science Museum
where we're committed to making science accessibleto all.

Over the course of human history, science has developed from our early understanding of fire, wind, water, and earth to exploring everything from galaxies far away to the very building blocks of life itself. The aim of our museum is to create a space where everyone can experience the wonders of our discoveries and perhaps even ignite a lifelong passion to continue the exploration of the world around us.

FEEL INVIGORATED

DISCOVER THE FINEST DETAILS

We believe science should not be confined to the textbook, but brought to live through exhibits. This is why we have over 1000 different exhibits on the many varied subjects of science to explore. Many of these exhibits are designed for you to interact with and play around to see science come to life (apart from the dinosaur exhibits – they only come to life at night when everyone’s gone home).

EXHIBITION

MAMMOTHS EXHIBITION

Explore the Ice Age world of mammoths at our Science Museum exhibition. Get up close to these colossal creatures, discover their secrets, and marvel at their adaptations for survival. Don't miss this captivating glimpse into prehistoric life.

INTERACTIVE GALLERY

Enhance your students' learning with a visit to our Interactive Gallery at the Science Museum. Our engaging exhibits and guided tours led by educators offer hands-on experiences that complement classroom teachings. Spark curiosity and inspire exploration in a dynamic educational environment.

HUMAN ORGANS EXHIBITION

Uncover the mysteries of the human body at our captivating exhibition. Dive into interactive displays and gain insights into the function and importance of organs like the heart, brain, and lungs. Experience the marvels of human physiology firsthand.
